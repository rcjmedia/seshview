SESHVIEW

Synchronized playback, watch videos with your friends, synchronized press/play at the same time.

Welcome. To get started, create a Room where you'll enter the YouTube URL.

Once the Room is created, click on the Room title and share the Room URL to your friends, co-worker, etc.

Test it by opening two different web browsers (Chrome and Firefox), paste the same room url to the address bar and press Play.

Enjoy!
